About & Intellect

I am a researcher, writer, and law student investigating the intersections of nationalism, cultural evolution, and political theory.

Through **Suyodhana**, I try to construct an archival framework where ideas are decoupled from transient online timelines and treated as active, permanent inquiries. My long-term projects explore the architectural history of institutions, legal structures within South Asia, and the preservation of high-grade typographic environments online.
